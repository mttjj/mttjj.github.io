## Note

The compendium collections WildC.A.T.s \#0-13, Special \#1, Trilogy \#1-3, Sourcebook \#1-3, and more. However, the book is mapped in chronological order by story and many issues contain more than one story. So two stories from issue 7 (for example) may come in completely different parts of the book. As such, I’m tracking the reading of this compendium by page number, similar to standard prose books, instead of issue number.
